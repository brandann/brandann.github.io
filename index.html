In my time at Northshore I developed my skills in management, customer interactions, schedules and deadlines, product engineering, and professional communication.

I spend much of my free time studying new topics in computer science and business, not covered in school. My goal is to always be up to date and with most current technologies, to improve my never-ending study of Computer Science.
