Nybble Studios

Interactive Imagination Redefined



Nybble Studios is both a game development and publishing company. It was founded in 2015 in the greater Seattle area. Its first commercial release was GhostLight Manor, a collaboration with the University of Washington. Nybble is dedicated to creating experiences that entertain first and drive revenue second. Every member of the Nybble family is a gamer, which imbues the company with a vision that surpasses its size.
